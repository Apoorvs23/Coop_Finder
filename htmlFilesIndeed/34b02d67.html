Position Overview:

We are in search of a Front-End or Full-Stack Developer for a part-time remote contract role, requiring approximately 20 hours of work per week. The successful candidate will have experience in developing projects from scratch, a solid grasp on front-end frameworks, and familiarity with design tools. Your expertise in responsive design, combined with your knowledge of cloud platforms, will be pivotal in our projects.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and maintain web applications using frameworks such as React, Angular, Vue.js.

- Collaborate with our back-end team to design, integrate, and deploy applications on cloud platforms like AWS or Azure.

- Ensure top-notch user experiences through responsive design and effective API integrations.

- Partner with UI/UX designers to transition designs into dynamic web interfaces.

- Optimize applications for efficiency, scalability, and security.

Experience:
- Strong proficiency in JavaScript and front end framework

- Experience with React.js and Redux for building user interfaces

- Familiarity with front-end build tools.

- Knowledge of back-end technologies like AWS.

- Experience working with version control systems like Git

- Familiarity with Agile development methodologies and tools like Jira

- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail

- Excellent communication and collaboration skills
