Title: Power Co-op - Mississauga


Position Summary:

Hatch is seeking Power Co-op Students with an interest in Energy and Power Systems for 4-month, 8-month or 12-month internships starting in Summer 2024. You will work on a variety of client-driven projects to support all sectors within Power (nuclear, thermal, and eGrid which encompasses renewables, hybrid power, transmission & distribution and grid modernization).


What a Co-op at Hatch Will Offer You:


You will:


  • Be given opportunities to contribute to complex projects in meaningful ways
  • Work on problems and gain experience in the office and on project sites
  • Collaborate with diverse teams locally and globally \
  • Work in an exciting environment where you can apply your academic learning on a daily basis
  • Benefit from informal mentorship from some of the leading minds and authorities in the industry
  • Participate in local lunch and learns, Student Showcase, Sustainability Week, Toastmasters, and more to develop your skills
  • Join in charitable, social, and sports activities with other Hatch employees


We’re entrepreneurs with a technical soul. Our flat organizational structure creates an atmosphere of openness and participation. We build practical solutions that are safe, innovative, and sustainable. Our ultimate goal is to achieve no harm for our people, our clients, and our communities.


Responsibilities:


  • Solve challenging problems pertaining to energy technologies and power generating systems
  • Work in a multi-disciplinary engineering environment
  • Actively participate in the creation of the technical reports and presentations to clients
  • Assist in the research and development of energy technologies


Education and Experience:


  • Preference for 3rd or 4th year Engineering student
  • Strong academic record
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills


Competencies (knowledge, skill, attributes):


  • Good understanding of engineering and business fundamentals as they apply to engineering problems and projects
  • Ability to learn quickly and to take on new challenges
  • Collaborative team player with excellent interpersonal skills
  • Excellent organizational skills and attention to detail
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Shows critical and creative thinking and problem solving, and brings forward solutions for our clients' toughest challenges
  • Highly motivated, with a positive attitude and an ability to understand innovation
  • Adapts and embraces change


To be considered, you must submit: resume and unofficial or official transcript.


Why join us?

  • Work with great people to make a difference
  • Collaborate on exciting projects to develop innovative solutions
  • Top employer


What we offer you?


  • Flexible work environment
  • Long term career development
  • Think globally, work locally
