Ready to Make an impact?

The Pharmacy Intern is responsible to provide superior customer service while completing the essential training to become a licensed Pharmacist. The student will gain a progressive learning experience in all aspects of pharmacy operations, administration, customer care, Profile programs and professional services. Actively contributes to an environment of employee and customer engagement.

Here’s where you’ll be focusing:

Job Description :

  • Professional pharmacy activities appropriate to level of education.
  • Prescription Assembly – Data Entry.
  • Process Customer Transactions, Receive and Release Prescriptions.
  • Answer Phones.
  • Promotion of all Profile Programs.
  • Compounding.
  • Pill Pack Assembly.
  • Filing Prescriptions, Ordering and replenishing supplies, cleaning.
  • Drug order creation and receiving.
  • Adhere to and implement all applicable company standards.
  • Other duties as assigned.

What you have to offer:

Job Requirements :

  • Must be a pharmacy student at an accredited Pharmacy program (4th year student).
  • Flexible availability.
  • Injection certified at the time of interview | hire.
  • Registered with College of Pharmacists as an intern.

Technical Skills :

  • Above average communication skills (both oral and written).
  • Strong organizational skills with attention to detail.
  • Ability to be self-directed and to work independently in meeting strict deadlines will be essential.

Education and Experience :

  • Enrollment in Pharmacy program from a recognized institution.
  • Previous pharmacy experience a definite asset.
