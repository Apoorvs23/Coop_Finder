      IPEX is one of the North American leading providers of advanced plastic piping systems. Our mission is to shape a better tomorrow by connecting people with water and energy.


      We currently have an exciting opportunity as a Buyer Co-Op Student. This role is based in our Oakville Office in and reports to the Manager, Indirect Purchasing.


      Do not miss the opportunity to join a diverse group of people-centric professionals, thought leaders and rapid thinkers, entrepreneurs in spirit and status quo-fighters!


      Job Summary


      The Co-Op buyer will assist in performing purchases consistent with parameters set out by the budgetary and forecasting exercises to support business needs. The incumbent will work towards finding opportunities and implementing initiatives to improve site efficiencies. This role responds to the needs for procurement of goods and services to support site’s operations & productivity.


      Requirements


      Principal Responsibilities


      • Assisting with Site Purchasing activities, specifically for Indirect Items
      • Assisting with Inventory Management
      • Finds opportunities and implements initiatives to improve site efficiencies
      • Overlooking supplier performance, special attention should be given to delivery dates
      • Keeping Plants abreast of potential supply chain risks
      • Assisting with Procurement of Indirect Items (Industrial, Safety & Janitorial Supplies, Waste Management, Oils & Lubricants) as requested
      • Observing and analyzing KANBAN/Min-Max to run inventory levels
      • Coordinating between 3 departments: Procurement / Accounts payables / Receiving
      • Collaborating with team members on different projects as needed


      Qualifications & Experience


      Education & Certificates


      • Being currently enrolled in post graduate studies in Supply Chain Management or in Business Administration
      • A SCMP / CPM accreditation is considered an asset


      Technical Skills & Experience


      • Intermediate to Advanced knowledge of Microsoft Office, specifically Excel
      • Knowledge of sourcing & procurement techniques
      • Experience using various MRP/ERP system
      • Experience in supply chain or manufacturing field would be an asset


      Competencies:


      • Excellent negotiation and collaboration skills
      • Well-developed organization and prioritization skills; detailed oriented
      • Strong analytical, decision-making & problem-solving skills
      • Attention to details
      • Great Communication Skills
