      IKO Industries Ltd. is a market leader in the manufacturing of roofing and building materials. IKO is a Canadian owned and operated business with production facilities worldwide and has many years of unparalleled success in the roofing materials industry. Quality, integrity, and trustworthiness are the values that underlie this success, and we have built this company by hiring people who hold these values. People like you!

      Job Description

      Co-op Student is primarily responsible for helping and supporting project team.

      Job Requirements

      • Assist Project Engineers with the coordinated executed of projects. Typical projects would include infrastructure upgrades and improvements to systems that support production enhancement of quality and/or quantity.
      • Help with the testing/commissioning of newly installed equipment and machines to ensure the fulfillment of contract specifications
      • Creation of project schedules, monitors progress, and create summarized status reports.
      • Develop detailed budget reports and presentations for senior management and ownership.
      • Develop/update project management frameworks to improve efficiency
      • Office Tasks

      Desired Skills And Experience

      • Seeking a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical or Manufacturing/Industrial Engineering
      • Strong written, verbal, presentation, and interpersonal skills and the ability to work effectively in a team is required
      • Multi-disciplinary knowledge of engineering and an ability to innocate using practice, economical solutions and/or strategies
      • Ability to work independently with little/no supervision
      • Experience in Autodesk Inventor and AutoCAD
      • Competent in MS software including MS Projects
      • Experience in a high speed continuous process manufacturing background is preferred.

      Benefits of Employment: IKO recognizes that its success is due to the strength of its employees. A primary goal of IKO is to promote individual employee's sense of accomplishment and contribution so that employees enjoy their association with IKO. The Company invests in its employees so that they are the most knowledgeable in the industry, and undertakes great efforts to nurture loyalty to, and teamwork at, IKO. We are pleased to offer competitive compensation, health care, a progressive and challenging workplace and a commitment to teamwork and integrity.

We are committed to diversity and inclusion and are pleased to consider all qualified applicants for employment without consideration to race, religion, creed, color, national origin, age, gender, sexual orientation, marital status, veteran status or disability.
