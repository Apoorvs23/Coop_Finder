      Talent Acquisition Specialist at Empire Communities - Creating Experiences That Matter

      Creating Communities Beyond the Home.

      We are one of North America’s largest private homebuilders, with locations in Ontario, Texas, Georgia, the Carolinas and Tennessee. Our vision is to continue to grow, innovate and Create Communities & Experiences That Matter for both homeowners and our team.

      There is no set path at Empire; we offer you the opportunity to grow in ways you never imagined. Your experiences at Empire will take you where you want to grow.

      We are hiring an Accounting Intern for a 4 month contract starting in January!

      This Accounting Internship will give you a hands-on, general accounting experience working with the accounting and tax teams on monthly and quarterly reporting, performing reconciliations as well as reporting on assigned General Ledgers, journal entry posting, and financial statement drafting. You will assist with GST and HST reporting, tax compliance, and other administrative tasks.

      What You Bring:

      • Currently enrolled in a program within Accounting, Finance, or related field
      • Skilled using Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, and PowerPoint), Outlook
      • Strong analytical/organizational skills with the ability to prioritize workload to meet tight deadlines in a fast-paced and dynamic work environment

      Why Join Empire for your internship?

      At Empire Communities, it’s important to have a genuine connection with the work you do. We believe our employees and co-op students bring meaningful stories and experiences that serve as inspiration to do great work and build communities that matter.

      Our Accounting team offers a unique experience in that you’ll have the opportunity to collaborate and learn from some of the most talented professionals in the industry.
